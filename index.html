Good To Know

Frequently asked questions.

What is a VIN verification?

It's an inspection where a licensed verifier confirms your vehicle's VIN matches its documents and records it on the official form — commonly required for out-of-state vehicles, specially constructed or salvage vehicles, and certain title transactions.

What should I have ready?

The vehicle itself, its title or registration paperwork, and a photo ID. Dealerships should have the relevant transaction documents on hand.

How long does it take?

Most verifications are completed on site in about 15–20 minutes.

How are travel fees calculated?

The $40 base fee applies to every verification. Mobile service adds $50 within 5 miles ($90 total) or $100 within 20 miles ($140 total) of our Lake Elsinore office. Beyond 20 miles is quoted by mileage at the verifier's discretion.

Do you serve dealerships?

Yes. We work with both private-party owners and dealerships, and we offer volume scheduling for lots and fleets.

Can you verify any vehicle?

If it has a VIN — cars, trucks, RVs, motorhomes, OHVs, trailers, motorcycles and more — we can verify it.
